what is anti matter

Anti-matter is a type of matter that is composed of antiparticles, more precisely anti electrons and anti protons, which have the same mass as particles of ordinary matter but opposite charge.

how is anti matter created

Anti-matter is created in high-energy particle collisions, such as those that occur in accelerators or in cosmic ray interactions.

where can anti matter be found

Anti-matter can be found in certain cosmic phenomena, such as pulsars and black holes, and is also produced in laboratories on Earth.
